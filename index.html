Founded BigLoveFinder™ 2023

Bill Lipsit

Founder & CEO · BigLoveFinder™

Bill Lipsit is a big man with an even bigger vision. For years, Bill watched his best friend Jake navigate the dating world with nothing but charisma, a denim jacket, and an unsettling amount of confidence. And yet — somehow — Jake wasn't finding matches.

Bill tried everything. He wingmanned at bars. He set Jake up with coworkers. He once made a poster. None of it worked at scale. Then one day, staring at a Whopper and thinking deeply about life, it hit him: there are women out there who specifically love a big guy — they just had no good way to find one.

BigLoveFinder™ was born. Not from market research. Not from investor decks. From one man's absolute refusal to let his friend go on a diet.

"Jake didn't need to change. The algorithm needed to change. So I built the algorithm."

— Bill Lipsit, Founder & CEO

The Exact Moment BigLoveFinder™ Was Invented

Bill and Jake were sitting inside a Burger King. Jake was explaining — for the fourteenth time — why he didn't need to go to the gym. Bill was nodding. A woman walked by, looked at Jake, and smiled. Jake didn't notice because he was looking at the menu.

Bill watched this happen and thought: "There has to be a better system." He went home. He built BigLoveFinder™. Jake still doesn't know that woman smiled at him. Bill has decided never to tell him — it makes the founding story better.
