HOW NOZEMPIC™ WORKS

Three simple steps to a more aerodynamic face.

⚠️ Important Safety Info

KNOWN SIDE EFFECTS

We are legally required to tell you these. Please do not let this stop you.

😤

Reduced Snoring Volume

Partners report this as the only positive side effect. We agree.

🌬️

Decreased Wind Resistance

Patients walk slightly faster. Some report being overtaken by their own hats.

🤧

Phantom Nostril Syndrome

Occasional sensation of a larger nose that is no longer there. Therapy available.

😵

Mild Identity Crisis

Patients may not recognize themselves in photos for up to 6 weeks. Exciting for most.

👓

Glasses Slippage

Reduced nose bridge may cause glasses to slide. Nose pads sold separately.

🐶

Temporary Smell Reduction

Dogs may no longer recognize you. This has nothing to do with the injection site.

Serious Warning: Do not use Nozempic™ if you are a professional wine taster, a bloodhound, or if your nose is load-bearing. Ask your doctor if Nozempic™ is right for you. Ask your nose too, out of respect.
